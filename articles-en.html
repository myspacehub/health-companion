Today's Method: finish one practical action

Do not chase perfection today. Pick one small action: add protein, take a 10-minute walk after a meal, reduce screen stimulation before bed, or save one complete log.

Protein Estimate: eat enough without weighing everything

Use practical portions: one egg is about 6 g protein, one cup of milk about 8 g, a palm-sized portion of cooked meat or fish about 20-25 g, and one serving of tofu about 10-15 g.

Sleep Recovery: stabilize bedtime rhythm

When sleep is low, start with a realistic bedtime, reduce screen stimulation 30 minutes before bed, and use calming audio or breathing practice. For ongoing severe insomnia, consult a qualified clinician.

Digestion: support absorption before adding more food

If digestion feels unstable, avoid suddenly increasing meal size. Try smaller meals, gentle snacks, a 10-minute post-meal walk, and note which foods feel comfortable.

Small Activity Goals: low friction beats high pressure

Start with 5 minutes of stretching, a 10-minute walk, or one light strength session. Health Companion turns small actions into visible trends.

Long-term Logging: make health reviewable

Weight, sleep, protein, activity, and digestion are foundation signals. Logging is not about pressure; it helps you notice patterns earlier and adjust more calmly.
