Health Companion Privacy Policy

简体中文 · 繁體中文 · Home

Effective date: July 4, 2026

Health Companion is a local-first personal health tracking and habit review tool. The app follows data minimization: no account system, no advertising, no cross-app tracking, and no upload of your health records to a developer server.

Data handled by the app

You may manually record or import weight, sleep, heart rate, resting heart rate, steps, active energy, workouts, protein intake, energy level, digestion status, and notes. The app also stores goals, reminder times and messages, language, theme, font size, wellness favorites, completed practice history, and sleep-audio timer and loop settings on your device.

Apple Health

With your explicit permission, the app reads body mass, sleep analysis, heart rate, resting heart rate, step count, active energy, and workout data from Apple Health to populate daily records, trends, and review suggestions. The app does not write data to Apple Health.

The app may automatically sync today's Apple Health data when it launches or returns to the foreground, and it also provides a manual import button. You can revoke Apple Health access at any time in iOS Settings or the Health app.

Storage and transfer

Health records and settings are stored locally by default. The developer does not upload your health records to a server, use them for advertising, sell them, or track you across apps. When you intentionally import, export, or share a file, the data is transferred to the location or app you choose. iOS or iCloud may back up app data according to your system settings; that is handled by Apple system services.

Notifications

With permission, the app uses iOS local notifications for health reminders. Reminder times, repeat rules, and reminder text remain on your device. Health reminders are not system alarms, and sound or display may be affected by system permissions, Silent Mode, Focus, or Scheduled Summary.

Wellness content, sleep audio, and third-party webpages

Sleep audio is packaged locally or played from files available in the app. Playback state, timers, and completion history remain on your device. The Wellness section may show entries from Bilibili, YouTube, other third-party video platforms, organization webpages, or browser pages. Stable direct videos may play in the app; search pages, channels, and organization webpages show a source note first, then open in the system browser. The app does not download, copy, or host those third-party videos and does not actively send your health records to those pages. Third-party pages may process network address, cookies, sign-in state, browser information, or usage information under their own privacy policies.

Deletion and backup

You can clear local records in Settings or remove local data by deleting the app. CSV and JSON backups are created only when you request them; exported files are under your control.

Advertising, analytics, and tracking

The current version contains no advertising SDKs, uses no third-party analytics SDKs, does not track you across apps or websites, does not sell personal data, and does not use HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or data mining.

Medical boundary

Scores, trends, nutrition, sleep, exercise, and wellness suggestions support general habit tracking only. They are not medical diagnosis, treatment advice, medication advice, or emergency services. If you experience serious, persistent, or sudden symptoms, consult a qualified healthcare professional or contact local emergency services.
